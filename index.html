Key Features

Advanced Booking System

Listeo includes a built-in and free advanced booking system that offers complete
control over online reservation methods and QR code verification system.

QR Code Verification System

Allow listing owners to verify visitor tickets using their smartphones. By scanning
a QR code, owners can quickly confirm ticket authenticity directly from their
dashboard, making event entry more efficient.

If a customer selects cash payment, the ticket will show as ‘unpaid’ until settled,
allowing owners to track payment status with ease.

QR verification system works with all booking methods

  • Rental
  • Services
  • Events
  • Classifieds

Rental

e.g. vehicles, apartments or products Users can choose check in & out date 📅, number of guests 🤵 or extra service 💰 and send a
request to the host to book a reservation. Once the host accepts request user can be charged
for the entire amount at reserved time (payments are processed through WooCommerce).
  • Availability Calendar (single and multi-days)
  • Hourly Booking (Start / End Time - helpful for renting cars, bikes etc.)
  • Regular, weekend and custom price per night or hour
  • Max. guests per reservation and minimum stay
  • Mandatory Fees (e.g. cleaning service)
  • Instant Booking (without owner approval)
  • Extra Paid Services
  • Children & Animals (custom prices)
  • QR Code Verification System

Services

e.g. restaurants, barbers, doctors Users can reserve spots at e.g. restaurants 🍔, barbers 🧔, etc. They can choose
date and time slot or specific hour ⏲️ if time slots are non set - in this case time
picker will be displayed in the booking widget instead of time slots and guests will be
able to make reservations only in opening hours range. Everything from rental method plus:
  • Availability Calendar (single day)
  • Time Slots or Time Picker

Events

e.g. concerts, conferences Online ticketing system 🎫 for any type of venue or event.
Users can add event listings on your website while also sell tickets for those events.
  • Start & End Date
  • Ticket Price
  • Max. Available Tickets
  • Pricing Menu
  • Extra Services

Classifieds

Let people sell new or used products Advertising feature that brings buyers and sellers together when someone wants
to sell a car, buy new patio furniture, buy new smartphone or find a new babysitter
for his kid. 🙂

Split Payments

When the payment for booking is made Listeo splits the payment
between the vendor and site admin (you). You automatically receive
commission and vendor his share so you don't need to bother with
manual payouts!

This feature is optional. You can use any payment gateway but split
payment works only with Stripe
